What role does the atmosphere play?

Imagine a place where the sunlight burns the human skin in couple of minutes, and the rocks become so hot that water boils on them. In the night the area becomes so cold that CO2 gas turns into dry ice; Moon is such a place! Those difficult conditions caused by the lack of Atmosphere.

But on Earth, the atmosphere, just like a protective cover, prevents too much sun radiation from entering into the planet and, creates a suitable condition for mankind living.
