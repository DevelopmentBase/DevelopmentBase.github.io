In most areas of Earth, the percentage of water vapor in the air varies from one to three, and in some conditions, it might be up to 5 percent.

Different human activities increase the density of CO2 and other gases inside the atmosphere. In addition to these gases, human activities may add new materials to the atmosphere and change the quality of the air.
