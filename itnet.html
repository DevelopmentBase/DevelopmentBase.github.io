IT & Networking Summary

In the role of IT, my task was monitoring and troubleshooting all systems which were connected to the network. Managing a network means managing the interconnection as well as the entire systems consuming the network bandwidth. I have the experience where a network infrastructure included servers, computers, printers, IP phones, security cameras, fingerprint devices, firewall and VPN devices plus, the network devices themselves, routers and switches.

Some of tasks that I have done was troubleshooting network devices, or reconfiguring their settings. Troubleshooting systems connected to the network and software installed on them. Deplying and replacing new hardware systems.
